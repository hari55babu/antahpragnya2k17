RGUKT Basar's

Antah Pragnya 2K17




Antah Pragnya, the national level technical extravaganza, organized by RGUKT, is an ode to the stupendous growth in science and technology over past two years. It is a platform for students across the country to showcase their talent through technically challenging and mind blogging events. This fiesta focuses on students effort towards gaining experience that exhilarates and triggers their curiosity.
